About BH Solutions

BH Solutions is a family-led automotive and transportation center serving Oroville, CA and surrounding communities. The goal is simple: make life easier for drivers by putting vehicles, U-Haul, ignition interlocks, registration help, logistics support, and trusted referrals in one place.

Through Alliance Property Network, BH Solutions also acts as a real estate liaison, connecting clients to licensed agents across the country for home purchases, sales, and relocations.

Whether you’re buying your next vehicle, dropping off a U-Haul, getting an IID installed, or looking for a real estate referral, BH Solutions is designed to be your one-stop local hub.
