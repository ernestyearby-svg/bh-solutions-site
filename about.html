About BH Solutions

BH Solutions is a family-led automotive and transportation center serving Oroville, CA and the surrounding communities. The business was built to simplify life for drivers — one trusted location for vehicles, registration, U-Haul rentals, ignition interlocks, diagnostics, and logistics.

Through our partnership with Alliance Property Network, BH Solutions also acts as a real estate liaison, connecting clients to licensed agents across the country for home purchases, sales, and relocations.

Whether you’re buying your next vehicle, dropping off a U-Haul, getting an IID installed, or looking for a real estate referral, BH Solutions is designed to be your one-stop hub.
