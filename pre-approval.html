Pre-Approval Made Simple

Pre-approval at BH Solutions is a conversation, not pressure. We walk through income, work history, and budget so you know what payment range makes sense before you pick a vehicle.

Call for Pre-Approval Text Your Questions

What to Bring

  • Valid driver’s license
  • Proof of income (paystubs, award letters, etc.)
  • Proof of residence (bill or document with your address)
  • Insurance information, if available

What We Look At

  • Stability of income and length of employment
  • Down payment amount you’re comfortable with
  • Vehicle price range and payment comfort zone
  • Past auto history and any open loans

How the Conversation Works

  1. We review your income and bills together.
  2. We talk through realistic payment ranges.
  3. We match that range to the vehicles on the lot.
  4. We explain your options clearly before you sign anything.

Every situation is different. The goal is to keep the payment comfortable and the process transparent.
