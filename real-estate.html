Alliance Property Network Real Estate Referrals

BH Solutions is more than automotive and transportation — we are also a real estate liaison through Alliance Property Network (APN).

As an APN partner, BH Solutions connects clients with trusted, licensed real estate professionals in all 50 states for:

  • Buying or selling a home
  • Investment and rental properties
  • Relocation and out-of-state moves
  • First-time homebuyers who need guidance

You work with BH Solutions locally, and we introduce you to a vetted APN partner agent in the market where you need help. No extra cost to you — the referral process is handled behind the scenes.

How it works:

  1. Tell us where you’re buying, selling, or relocating.
  2. We match you with a licensed APN partner agent in that area.
  3. The APN agent contacts you, provides guidance, and stays available throughout your move.
